Banana Bread

Description

Banana Bread is a classic american staple first popularized in the 1930s.
A cake like quick bread this dish is a light, moist, and fluffy cake bread that goes well alone,
or you can pair it with ice cream to add some creamy contrast!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas
