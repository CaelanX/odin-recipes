Old Fashioned

Description

Old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugars with bitters an water and adding rye or bourbon.
The drink is usually served in a rocks glass and garnished with an orange rind and/or a cocktail cherry.

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 1 dash bitters
  • 2 fluid ounces whiskey (rye or bourbon)
  • 1 lemon twist
  • ice cubes
  • 1 orange slice, for garnish
  • 1 maraschino cherry, for garnish

Cooking Steps

  1. Muddle sugar cube, water, and bitters in an old fashioned glass for 1 minute.
  2. Pour in whiskey and stir for an additional minute
  3. Squeeze the lemon twist over the glass and drop it in and add the ice cubes.
  4. Garnish with a slice of orange and a maraschino cherry; serve with a swizzle stick.
  5. Enjoy!
