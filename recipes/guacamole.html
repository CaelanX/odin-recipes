Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a classic mexican avocado dip, spread, or salad.
This avocado based dip is great for chips, tacos, salads and anything else you can think to use it with.
Not only is this recipe amazingly good, it's also exceptionally easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Begin by dicing your onion and tomato and put them in a bowl to the side.
  2. Mince your garlic, chop the cilantro and juice your lime.
  3. Peel and pit your avocados into a pestle and mash them to a little bit chunkier then your desired end result
  4. Add your tomatoes, onions, salt, lime juice, cilantro, garlic and cayenne if desired.
  5. Mix till desired chunkiness and ingredients are distributed throughout.
  6. Enjoy!
